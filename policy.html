Last Updated: February 22, 2025

Introduction

Charles Stein (“we,” “our,” or “us”) respects your privacy. This policy explains how USB KVM Display Control (the “App”) handles your information. By using the App, you agree to this policy.

Data Collection

The USB KVM Display Control app does not collect, store, or transmit any personal or usage data, including but not limited to:

The app operates entirely locally on your device and does not require internet access.

Since no data is collected, none is used for any purpose.

Data Sharing

Security

The App does not store or process sensitive information. However, we prioritize user trust and ensure the App is designed to operate securely within your local system.
